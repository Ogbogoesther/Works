Frequently asked questions

Fashion design is the art of creating clothing and accessories. It involves sketching, fabric selection, pattern making, and garment construction to create unique and stylish pieces.

You can place an order through our website by browsing our collection and selecting your desired pieces. For custom designs, you can contact us directly for a consultation.

The turnaround time for custom designs typically ranges from 2-6 weeks, depending on the complexity of the design and fabric availability. We will provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.

We accept returns and exchanges within 14 days of receiving your order. The item must be in unused condition with original tags and packaging. For custom designs, returns or exchanges are not accepted unless there is a manufacturing defect.
